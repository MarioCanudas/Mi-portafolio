# about me
I am an actuary in training with one obsession: building and using tools that are as minimalist as they are powerful. I enjoy combining actuarial rigor with a modern tech stack. I am constantly exploring new tools, but I only adopt those that provide real value and simplicity, filtering out the noise of the purely superficial.

My main driver is continuous learning. More than just a habit, learning has become my primary hobby; there is nothing more rewarding than the technical challenge of mastering a library or solving a complex probability problem.

I don't look for the easiest path, but the most elegant and creative one. I am passionate about tackling challenges from scratch, yet I possess the pragmatism to implement robust, existing solutions when project efficiency demands it.

- **languages**: python, sql, r.
- **frameworks**: reflex (frontend), fastapi (backend).
- **data**: pandas, scikit-learn, numpy, tensorflow, traditional actuarial models.
- **tools**: excel, git, docker, linux.
